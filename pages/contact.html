How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while a comprehensive BI solution or enterprise app could take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! We offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. From 24/7 monitoring to regular updates and enhancements, we're here for the long term.

What is your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing based on project requirements. Options include fixed-price for well-defined projects, time & materials for evolving scopes, or dedicated team models for ongoing work. Contact us for a customized quote.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! We love working with startups and have flexible engagement models suited for early-stage companies. We can help you build MVPs, scale your product, and grow your technical capabilities.
